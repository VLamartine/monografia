About this site

The purpose of this site is to make available a random draw tool, transparent and verifiable. Our draw method, described below, guarantees that, for the same input (seed + items in the same order), the result will always be the same.

Weighted draw method

  1. The user enters which seed origin they desire
    If the origin is one of the available randomness beacons, the seed can be:
    • The last pulse (Default)
    • The next pulse
    • A pulse from a specific time from the past
  2. The User enters the items that they want ordered, one per line, with its weight
  3. Each item gets a chance that is equal to its weight divided by the total sum of the weight. With 4 decimal places
  4. Each item is placed in an interval from 0 to 100, and gets a subinterval equal to its chance
  5. The seed is divided by 2^512, and the item that has the result in its interval is the winner
